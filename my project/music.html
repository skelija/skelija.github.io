Childhood

To begin with, being only five years old I started to go to the musical school. There I learned how to sing, play piano and music theory. Not long after, I started going to composition lessons where I created my own music.

High school

After being in musical school for seven years I transfered to Klaipeda's Eduarda's Balsys gymnasium of art. There my specialty was conducting. I also took piano lessons as my second instrument, as well as jazz vocal lectures. While studying there I attended a few conducting competitions where I won. Also a few events where I sang in a chorus, duet or alone.

Presence

After I graduated from Klaipeda's Eduardas Balsys gymnasium of art, I did not pursue this as a career, although I had a good possibility for it. I always will have music as a piece of my heart. Maybe in the future I will keep music as my hobby, have a little chorus or just play piano for my own pleasure. So, if you have a song in mind, we can duet :)
