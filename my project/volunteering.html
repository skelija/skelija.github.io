Overall

I started volunteering at the age of fourteen in my high school students’ council. Also, I was a volunteer for three years in Lithuanian students’ union. There I was a part of communication, youth politics and marketing committees and a coordinator of the event organization committee. So if you have an interesting event idea - I would be happy to be a part of it!

High school student council

As I mentioned, I was a part of student’s council for a few years and then I became a president of this council. It was challenging but I always enjoy trying new things and overcoming my fears. I enjoyed teamwork and bringing a group of people to a team.

Lithuanian students union

Being a leader in students’ union was a big opportunity for me to be a part of politics including youth. Working with motivated students was a fulfilling experience as I always seek those.
